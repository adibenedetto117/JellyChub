Jellychub is a feature-rich Jellyfin client that puts your entire media library at your fingertips.

STREAMING

• Movies & TV Shows - Browse and stream your video library with full playback controls
• Music - Background playback with queue management
• Books - Built-in EPUB reader
• Live TV - Watch live television from your Jellyfin server
• Chromecast - Cast to compatible devices
• Picture-in-Picture - Keep watching while multitasking

MEDIA MANAGEMENT

• Jellyseerr - Discover and request movies and TV shows
• Radarr - View your movie queue, calendar, and manage your collection
• Sonarr - Track your TV show queue, calendar, and manage episodes

SERVER ADMINISTRATION

• Dashboard - Monitor active streams, transcoding, and server health
• Stream Control - Pause, stop, or kill active playback sessions
• User Management - Create, edit, and delete users
• Permissions - Configure library access, playback, and transcoding per user
• Scheduled Tasks - Run and monitor server maintenance tasks
• Server Control - Trigger library scans, restart, or shutdown

REQUIREMENTS

A Jellyfin server is required. Jellyseerr, Radarr, and Sonarr integrations are optional. Admin features require administrator privileges on your Jellyfin server.

Jellyfin is free, open-source media server software. Learn more at jellyfin.org
