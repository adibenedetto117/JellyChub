# Privacy Policy for JellyChub

**Last updated:** January 7, 2026

## Overview

JellyChub is a media player application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection

JellyChub does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or third parties. We have no servers that receive your information.

## Local Storage

The app stores data locally on your device only:

- **Preferences**: App settings, theme choices, and playback preferences
- **Downloads**: Media files you choose to save for offline use
- **Progress**: Playback position, reading progress, and bookmarks
- **Cache**: Temporary data to improve app performance

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Permissions

The app requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

- **Internet**: Required to stream media content
- **Storage**: Required to save downloaded files
- **Background playback**: Required to continue audio/video when the app is minimized

## Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs collecting your usage data.

## Data Security

All locally stored data uses your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device's security settings enabled.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/anthonyjarjar/jellychub/issues).
